How does AI for Blind work?

Blind for AI incorporates technologies such as deep learning, IOT and libraries such as opencv, tensorflow and keras to provide a priceless exerience to the blind for improving their interpersonal conversations.
The convutional network reads the faces of the people in front of the blind person using a pair of spectacles worn by the blind person and identifies their emotion out of the 7 elaborate emotions such as happy, sad, depressed, surprise, neutral and so on and communicates that to the blind person, through a earpiece. This helps improve the way the communicate.
